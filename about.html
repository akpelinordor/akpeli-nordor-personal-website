About

Akpeli Nordor.

PharmD-PhD • Cancer Scientist • Techbio Founder • Boston & Paris

A scientist and founder building at the intersection of precision health and AI.

In 2019, he co-founded Epigene Labs, a techbio company pioneering AI-powered data curation for precision oncology R&D. He built its core scientific team in France before relocating to Boston to develop the company's business in its primary market. Over seven years, Epigene Labs contributed to over 15 R&D programs, fueled more than 1,000 scientific explorations in the fight against cancer, and raised over €10M across venture and grant financing.

Prior to Epigene, Akpeli conducted research in cancer biology at Institut Curie in Paris and in computational biology at Massachusetts General Hospital in Boston — learning how disease works at the molecular level before deciding to change how it's fought at the systems level.

He holds a dual PharmD–PhD from Université Paris Cité and completed postdoctoral training at Harvard Medical School.

Alongside his research, he significantly contributed to the founding of Hello Tomorrow, a Paris-based non-profit promoting deeptech entrepreneurship worldwide.

Akpeli is now based in Boston, actively exploring what comes next at the edge of precision health and AI.
